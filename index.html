Get Keywords

My wife is a copywriter (and amazing). She needed to extract keywords from various industry leader's copy to use for blogs, social media, and SEO. I created this notebook to do just that. Enter the web URL's to crawl, then receive the top 'n' words. It uses Beautiful Soup for web crawling and scikit-learn for word tokenization and term frequency-inverse document frequency (tf-idf) analysis.
